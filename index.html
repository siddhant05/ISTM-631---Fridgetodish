Faq

Questions and Answers

What is the price for using this website?

The website is absolutely free to use! Just sign up with your email, select your ingredients and start preparing your recipes.

Why do I need to register?

You can access the majority of our services without having to register. Some services do allow you to personalise your experience in some way and you will need to register to leave comments, share your own recipes and save your favorite recipes.

How can I create my profile?

The easiest way to create your profile is to click on the login/ sign up button at the top of homepage. The sign up page asks for your name, email address and the password you want to set. You will receive an email to confirm your account

What if I forget my password?

If you have forgotten your password, you can ask for it to be reset. Click “Forgotten password?” on our sign in form, enter your email address, and we will send you instructions by email on how to reset it.

How can I save my favorite recipes?

You need to register with us to save your favorite recipes. After registering successfully, you can click on the save recipe button to save the recipes that you like. When you enter your profile, you can view all the recipes that you have saved.

How can I share my recipes?

When you select the ingredients, website will show you the list of all possible recipes that match with your ingredients. If you want to share any recipe, you would find a share recipe button below the list of recipes through which you can share or suggest us any recipe we should add for those ingredients.
